SOCIAL MEDIA DO'S AND DONT'S


DO'S
1. Think before posting any content.
2. Verify information before sharing.
3. Use privacy settings to protect data.
4. Communicate respectfully online.
5. Use social media for learning and growth.





DONT'S
1. Don't share personal or sensitive information.
2. Don't spread fake news or rumors.
3. Don't accept unknown requests blindly.
4. Don't engage in cyberbullying or trolling.
5. Don't waste excessive time scrolling.
