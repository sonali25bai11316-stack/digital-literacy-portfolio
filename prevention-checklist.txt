STAY SAFE ONLINE CHECKLIST



1. Use strong, unique passwords for every account

2. Enable 2-Factor Authentication (2FA)

3. Never share OTP, PIN, or passwords with anyone

4. Check links before clicking (Phishing awareness)

5. Use only trusted Wi-Fi networks

6. UPI Safety: Never approve unknown payment requests

7. UPI Safety: Double-check receiver details before sending money

8. Install apps only from official sources
